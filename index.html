about us

The Hydeout is one of Bhubaneswar’s most happening cafés and your go-to place for fun, food and fumes. Life is busy and we at Hydeout go the extra mile to help you beat the Monday blues, unwind from the daily hassles or party on the weekends. Be it succulent food to entice your taste buds or exotic flavors of hookah to take you to cloud nine or great music to shake a leg, we have it all for you to lighten up – a perfect hideout to enjoy with friends and family!
